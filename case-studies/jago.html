Introduction

Jago reimagines crypto management with a clean, intuitive mobile experience.

From buying and sending to swapping, earning, and exploring - every interaction is designed to be fast, secure, and beautifully simple. Your keys, your crypto.

Built for Better
Crypto Management

Problem

Crypto wallets overwhelm users with jargon, cluttered dashboards, and fragmented flows. Most feel like trading terminals - intimidating for newcomers and inefficient for experienced crypto users managing multiple chains.

Solution

Jago brings everything into one place - portfolio tracking, send/receive, swap, earn, and explore - with a dark-first UI, social login, and enough breathing room that complex Web3 actions feel approachable.

What the Research Revealed

We conducted in-depth research to improve the crypto wallet experience

Competitive audits of MetaMask, Rainbow, and Phantom - plus interviews with 8 crypto users - surfaced clear patterns around onboarding friction, network switching, and portfolio visibility.

82%

Users want a single dashboard for all chains and assets

74%

Onboarding drop-off linked to seed phrase anxiety and unclear auth paths

61%

Experienced crypto users want simplified send, swap, and network-switching flows

Reflection

What I learned

  • Onboarding is the first feature. Social login and plain-language copy reduced drop-off more than any portfolio chart or swap animation.
  • Two personas, one home screen. Daniel needed density; Amara needed calm. Progressive disclosure let both feel at home without a settings toggle.
  • Network switching must be invisible. Hiding chain complexity behind a single network pill cut support questions in usability tests.
  • Dark mode is a trust signal in Web3. A cohesive dark visual system made the wallet feel premium and reduced the "scam app" anxiety newcomers described.
