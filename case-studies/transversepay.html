Introduction

Making cross-border finance feel safe, simple, and premium.

TransversePay is a fintech landing page designed to help users move money globally with confidence - from a bold hero and onboarding steps to feature highlights, social proof, FAQs, and a final conversion push.

Interactive walkthrough

Reflection

What I learned

  • Trust before conversion. World ID security, live rates, and social proof had to appear early - not buried below the fold.
  • Three steps beat ten features. A simple signup → fund → send narrative converted better than a long feature grid.
  • Purple signals premium fintech. A bold violet hero with clean white sections made cross-border payments feel modern without crypto jargon.
  • FAQs close the loop. Addressing fees, security, and supported countries upfront reduced the anxiety users feel before clicking Get Started.
