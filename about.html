And honestly? I still believe there's more I can achieve. There's nothing I can't do as a designer - as long as I put my mind to it. Every "no" taught me something. Every scrapped frame made the next one sharper. Every collaboration reminded me why I chose this path. I'm not where I want to be yet - but I'm nowhere near where I started. And that gap? That's the whole point.

So if you're looking for someone hungry, coachable, weirdly optimistic, and serious about craft - hi. That's me. 👋
